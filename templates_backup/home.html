About This Project

With the rapid growth of digital healthcare systems, large volumes of medical data are continuously generated from patient records, clinical reports, laboratory results, imaging studies, and health monitoring devices. Analyzing this complex data using traditional methods is often challenging, time-consuming, and prone to errors, which can limit the ability of healthcare professionals and organizations to make effective and timely decisions. Data visualization and predictive analytics play a vital role in transforming these datasets into meaningful insights that support better understanding, early risk detection, and proactive intervention strategies.

This project focuses on systematically analyzing healthcare datasets and presenting the results through interactive visual dashboards integrated with predictive models. Advanced data visualization techniques represent historical health information in a clear and intuitive manner, while predictive models developed using machine learning forecast future health trends, patient outcomes, and potential risks. By combining visualization and predictive analytics in a single platform, the system enhances data-driven decision-making, improves accessibility of healthcare analytics, and empowers professionals to implement more effective patient care strategies.

How It Works

A comprehensive platform for healthcare data analysis, visualization, and risk prediction

📝

Data Collection & Input

Publicly available healthcare datasets are collected and processed. Users enter health parameters like BP, sugar, BMI, and lifestyle habits.

🧠

ML-Powered Prediction

Random Forest classifier performs feature selection, pattern recognition, and risk prediction with Accuracy, Precision, Recall & F1-Score evaluation.

📊

Interactive Visualization

Chart.js and Tableau dashboards present health trends, risk distributions, and analytics in clear, interactive visual formats.

🏥

Clinical Decision Support

Health tips, risk levels, probability scores, and downloadable reports empower professionals for better patient care strategies.

📈

Trend & History Tracking

Track prediction history over time, identify health trends, correlations, and potential risk indicators from past analyses.

🔒

Secure & Accessible

Bcrypt password hashing, OTP-based password recovery, and a responsive web-based interface for real-time interaction.
